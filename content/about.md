## About Me

I am a researcher at the **School of Ecology and Environment, Northwestern Polytechnical University**. My research focuses on **evolutionary biology**, **comparative genomics**, and **bioinformatics**.

## Research Interests

- Comparative genomics and evolutionary biology
- Single-cell analysis of vertebrate evolution
- Genome assembly and adaptation mechanisms
- Transcriptomic and proteomic analysis

## Research Highlights

My work explores the genetic mechanisms underlying major evolutionary transitions in vertebrates, including:

- **Liver evolution**: Single-cell analysis comparing amphioxus hepatic caecum with vertebrate liver
- **Lung evolution**: Tracing the origin and stepwise evolution of vertebrate lungs
- **Deep-sea adaptation**: Genomic mechanisms enabling hadal snailfish to survive extreme deep-sea environments
- **Limb loss**: Convergent regulatory elements in limbless amphibians and reptiles
