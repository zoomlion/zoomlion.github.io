Welcome to my new academic blog! I've decided to start this blog as a way to share my research journey, document interesting findings, and connect with the broader academic community.

## Why a Blog?

Academic blogs serve many purposes. They can help with:

- **Science communication** - Making research accessible to a wider audience
- **Personal branding** - Building an online presence in your field
- **Note-taking** - Documenting ideas and findings for yourself
- **Networking** - Connecting with other researchers

## What I'll Write About

Here are some topics I plan to cover:

1. Research updates and paper highlights
2. Tutorials on methods and tools
3. Thoughts on academia and career development
4. Conference attendances and takeaways

## Getting Started

This blog is built using **Jekyll** and hosted on **GitHub Pages**. The content is managed through **Decap CMS**, which allows me to write in Markdown without needing to edit HTML files directly.

> "The best time to start was yesterday. The next best time is today."

If you're an academic considering starting your own blog, I encourage you to give it a try. It doesn't have to be perfect to start - just begin and improve over time.

In future posts, I'll be sharing more about my research, useful resources I've discovered, and hopefully some helpful tutorials. Stay tuned!
