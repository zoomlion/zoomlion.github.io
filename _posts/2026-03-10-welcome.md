## Why Jekyll?

Jekyll is a static site generator that's perfect for blogs because:

1. **Fast** - No database, no dynamic processing
2. **Secure** - No vulnerable software to hack
3. **Free hosting** - Works great with GitHub Pages
4. **Markdown support** - Write content easily
